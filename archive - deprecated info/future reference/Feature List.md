# The Boritrader
**Author: Emmanuel**

The Boritrader is designed to facilitate automated crypto trading decisions on platforms like Binance. It is ideal for day traders looking to scan the entire market and for passive traders aiming to mitigate losses and pinpoint optimal entry points.

Currently, it supports the Binance API with plans to introduce additional features such as trailing stop loss and other advanced trading functionalities soon.

---

### 1. **Automated Trading**
   - **Description**: Execute trades automatically based on pre-configured algorithms and settings.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Customizable trading strategies.
     - Support for multiple algorithms (enable/disable as needed).
     - Automated trade execution (live and paper trading).

---

### 2. **Algorithmic Trading**
   - **Description**: Runs pre-built trading algorithms to find optimal buy/sell opportunities.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Easy algorithm integration (add/remove algorithms via user interface).
     - Run multiple algorithm threads simultaneously for diverse strategies.
     - Real-time strategy execution with notifications on trade opportunities.

---

### 3. **Multi-Ticker Support**
   - **Description**: Ability to monitor and trade across multiple cryptocurrencies simultaneously.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Add/remove tickers dynamically based on market trends.
     - Custom tickers list for personalized portfolio tracking.
     - Trade multiple pairs concurrently, scaling trade operations.

---

### 4. **Market Data Snapshot**
   - **Description**: Capture and store snapshots of market conditions for historical analysis.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Periodic market snapshots to identify historical trends.
     - View or compare market snapshots over time.
     - Use stored market snapshots for backtesting algorithms.

---

### 5. **Paper Trading Mode**
   - **Description**: Simulate trades in a risk-free environment without executing live trades.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Real-time simulated trades for strategy validation.
     - Switch between live and paper trading modes seamlessly.
     - Performance analysis of simulated trades to refine strategies.

---

### 6. **Live Trading**
   - **Description**: Execute real trades on the Binance exchange based on the bot's algorithms.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Real-time order placement and monitoring.
     - Trade logging for detailed performance reports.
     - Risk management through stop loss and trailing stop loss (SL/TSL) configurations.

---

### 7. **Customizable Configurations**
   - **Description**: Fine-tune bot settings for maximum trading efficiency.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Configurable trading frequency, asset pairing, and quantity.
     - Enable/disable advanced trading algorithms (e.g., Stop Loss, Trailing Stop Loss).
     - Notification settings for trade actions and market changes.

---

### 8. **Account Management & Info Retrieval**
   - **Description**: Fetches and displays Binance account details, balances, and trading history.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Real-time balance and asset information for informed decision-making.
     - Comprehensive account snapshot including trades, balance, and open positions.
     - Easy-to-read trading logs and history.

---

### 9. **Real-Time Market Streaming**
   - **Description**: Open live market data streams for real-time price monitoring.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Streaming of live prices for all enabled tickers.
     - Automatic event notification on price thresholds or algorithm conditions.
     - Multi-threaded streams for faster updates on different markets.

---

### 10. **Advanced Trade Logging**
   - **Description**: Logs detailed trade information (buy/sell actions, prices, profit).
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Exportable trade logs for further analysis or record-keeping.
     - Auto-summarized trade history showing key performance metrics.
     - Easy-to-read trade entries with key details like profit/loss, prices, and timestamp.

---

### 11. **Backtesting Support**
   - **Description**: Simulate trading strategies using historical market data.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Backtest multiple strategies across historical snapshots.
     - Analyze the performance of different algorithms on past market data.
     - Refine algorithms by testing against different market conditions.

---

### 12. **Order Book Analysis**
   - **Description**: Fetch and display detailed order book information for any trading pair.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Real-time order book tracking to identify buy/sell trends.
     - Visual order book depth for quick analysis of market liquidity.
     - Set triggers for large buy/sell orders that influence market movement.

---

### 13. **Trading Strategy Customization**
   - **Description**: Users can adjust parameters such as purchase price, trailing stop loss (TSL), and stop loss (SL) for enhanced control over trades.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Tailored trade execution based on user-defined rules.
     - Easy configuration of SL and TSL thresholds for each strategy.
     - Adaptive strategies based on real-time market conditions.

---

### 14. **Trade Notifications & Alerts**
   - **Description**: Notify users of important trade events and algorithmic triggers.
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Push notifications for buy/sell actions.
     - Real-time alerts for significant price changes or trade execution.
     - Custom notification settings for user preferences (e.g., only notify for large profits).

---

### 15. **Configurable Risk Management**
   - **Description**: Built-in risk management features like stop loss and trailing stop loss (TSL).
   - **Derivative Features**:
     - Automatic stop-loss execution to prevent large losses.

### 19. **Cross-Market & Multi-Asset Trading**

### 20. **User-Friendly Dashboard**
